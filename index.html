What this is

A pacing diary for people with ME/CFS. It helps you record activity and symptoms day by day, and look back over weeks and months for patterns you might otherwise miss.

Pacing is the central management strategy for ME/CFS. NICE guideline NG206 describes it as staying within an individual's energy limits to avoid triggering post-exertional malaise.

How it handles your data

Everything you enter is stored locally in this browser using localStorage. Nothing is sent to a server. There are no accounts, no tracking, and no analytics.

Two consequences of this:

  • If you clear your browser data, the diary clears with it.
  • The diary lives on one device. To move between devices, use the JSON backup and restore in the Export tab.

On phones, browser storage can occasionally be cleared by the operating system when space runs low. If you rely on the diary, download a JSON backup from the Export tab every week or two and keep it somewhere safe.

What it is not

This diary is not a diagnostic tool, not a treatment, and not a substitute for medical care. It is a record-keeping aid. Patterns it surfaces are observations, not conclusions.

If a sustained change in symptoms is worrying you, speak with your GP or specialist.
